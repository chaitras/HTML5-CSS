Comfort with a touch of luxury ♫♥♫

Us

We like to see our location as a modern haven in a space age world. We offer all the modern comforts like smart pins to provide access to locked areas, a fast complimentary WiFi, but still our interior reflects the comfort of an 19th century luxury inn. Space Bed-and-Breakfast has four guest rooms, a common lounge, and a dining area. Lunch and dinner are only available with prior reservation, but it can be well worth the small effort.

Rooms

Standard double

Our two standard double rooms come with a comfortable queen size bed, and a basic equipment.

Family room

As the name states the family room offers more space with a double bed and two single beds. For request a cot or a small extra bed is available. Ask for additional child or baby accessories during the reservation.

Honeymoon suite

Our honeymoon suite is a luxurious room with a large king size bed. The breakfast in bed includes also sparkling wine for those special moments.
